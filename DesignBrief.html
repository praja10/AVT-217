Bob’s Hell Burger Design Brief

Objective:

Create a website for Bob’s Hell Burger, a family owned American food restaurant. For this project, I want to put a twist on classic American burgers and make this restaurant have a more adventurous and spicy feel, one that matches the food. I hope to explore colors, typography, aesthetics and overall functionality in order to create a website for a unique restaurant.

Audience:

The primary audience for this restaurant would be people who are interested in this restaurant and want to explore the menu as well as the overall vibe of the place. The purpose of the website should be to entice more people to come and eat at the restaurant.

Competition:

Other burger joints in the area.

Look and Feel:

  • Warm
  • Appealing
  • Adventurous
  • Delicious
  • Devious
  • Spicy

Deliverables:

  • Restaurant Logo
  • Working restaurant Website

Site Pages:

  • Home
  • Menu
  • Merch
  • Order Online
  • Contact Us
  • Locations
  • Careers
